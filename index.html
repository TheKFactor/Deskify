Questions & answers

Before you open the workspace.

Is Deskify meant to replace ServiceNow?

This prototype is inspired by enterprise service desks, but it is intentionally lighter: fast triage, clean queues, ticket detail, and team visibility.

Can teams manage incidents and requests together?

Yes. The workspace includes incidents, requests, changes, priorities, categories, owners, status, and SLA state in one queue.

Does login create a real account?

For this local prototype, registration and login save a demo session in your browser only. No data is sent anywhere.

What happens after login?

You land in the Deskify workspace, where you can filter tickets, create new ones, inspect details, update status, and add timeline notes.
